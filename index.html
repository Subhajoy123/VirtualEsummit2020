About

Virtual E-Summit

E-summit or Entrepreneurship Summit is a fest conducted annually by Entrepreneurship Development Cell of NIT Durgapur to nurture young minds into various fields related to entrepreneurship and to promote the startup culture in India. It boasts of a plethora of events aimed solely to impart entrepreneurial knowledge and encourage budding entrepreneurs. E-Summit in the past years had been a grand success with iconic celeb-preneurs gracing our stage. Our big plans for this year were unfortunately postponed due to the ongoing pandemic. However, Team EDC explored new ways to bring something special to you! Join us on 20th and 21st June to experience the Largest Virtual Entrepreneurship Summit in India!
2 DAYS 1 CELEBRATION E-SUMMIT'20!
Mark your calendar! Clear out your schedule! Get ready to dive deep into the world of entrepreneurs - their struggles and rise to success. For more details just navigate your way through the website and find out what the grandest celebration of the year has in store for you !!
